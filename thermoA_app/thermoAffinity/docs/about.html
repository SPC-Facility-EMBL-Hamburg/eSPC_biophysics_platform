Objective

The main objective of ThermoAffinity is to obtain the binding affinity of a ligand (or protein) to a given protein by analyzing the thermophoresis shift (or initial fluorescence). ThermoAffinity is provided by the Sample Preparation and Characterization (SPC) Facility from the European Molecular Biology Laboratory (EMBL) Hamburg Station.
